Born from a simple question.

It started when a group of friends wanted to organize a weekend food drive. They quickly realized that managing spreadsheets, tracking attendance, and verifying volunteer hours was taking more time than the actual community service.

We realized that if we removed the friction of organizing, more people would step up to help. Today, SevaLog provides a frictionless, transparent way to connect willing hearts with communities in need.

The Principles We Live By

Guided by radical transparency, community focus, and frictionless action.

Trust Over Everything

Trust is the currency of volunteering. We verify every event, validate every hour, and ensure our data reflects actual community impact.

Frictionless Action

Good intentions shouldn't be blocked by bad software. We prioritize clean interfaces, fast registrations, and removing barriers to entry.

Impact Verification

Volunteering is work. We provide verified hours and build a credible portfolio of community service for every user.
