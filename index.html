How SevaLog Works

We make it incredibly simple to find causes you care about, track your personal impact, and build a verified portfolio of community service.

1. Register

Create your free volunteer profile in under 60 seconds. Tell us your skills and the causes you care most about.

2. Join an Event

Browse our live feed of upcoming community drives, food banks, and educational events happening near you.

3. Track Impact

Check in via the dashboard at the event. Earn verified hours, badges, and build a resume of your civic engagement.
